Our Services

Grant Writing

We specialize in finding government and state funding as well as private foundation grants to help both small and mid-size busniesses expand. Our specializations are researching funding for non-profits that focus on education, health and fitness, mentoring and other life-skills programs serving low income communities.

Consulting

Exponential Growth & Development supports individuals to help build and grow businesses, focusing on the fundamental aspects of the start up process. EGD ensures that businesses succeed from the very beginning.

CPR Training

Companies and individuals looking to re-certify or become certified in CPR, First Aid, and AED training required for work in the health care and child care industries can schedule a training through the calendar portal. Trainings are fun, easy, and last 2 years. Certifications are given through the American Red Cross. Classes start at $50 per person, and groups of 8 or more students receive a discount.
